The Office Sloth

Meet the Office Sloth...

  • Habitat: Office
  • Efficiency Factor: nan
  • Coffee Consumption: unlimited
  • Favourite Working Equipment: Home Slippers, Printer

They usually move at an average speed of 4 metres per minute, but can move at a marginally higher speed of 4.5 metres per minute if they are in immediate danger from a predator or Manager. Beside hanging out in meetings all day the Office Sloth usually hangs around the coffee machine. That's actuallyt inherited, because in free-living nature Sloths sometimes remain hanging from branches even after death.
